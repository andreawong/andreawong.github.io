Hello,

My name is Andrea Wong. I'm a technical writer and editor currently based out of Edmonton, Canada. I specialize in writing, editing, and designing documents to translate complex subject matter into digestable, accurate, navigable prose. I communicate with purpose and believe in reader-oriented writing.

Have a project in mind for me? Let's talk.

I'm not afraid

of the high-tech, the academic, the seemingly mundane, or the labyrinthine. I make it my business to thoroughly learn my subject matter from an informed reader's perspective so that I can fill in the gaps taken for granted by experts. I know what you want, I know what your readers need&emdash;and I make the text work for both of you.

I write and edit:

  • Help guides, user manuals, FAQs
  • Policy documents and training manuals
  • Product labels and packaging
  • Reports and funding proposals
  • Books and book chapters
  • Academic papers
